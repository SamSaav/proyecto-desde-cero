Mission

To support youth athletes living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who hace an interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn and practice skills related to the game of soccer, specifically those skills around team cooperation and good sportsmanship.

Vision

The Nothside Youth Soccer League aspires to develop strong, well-rounded, and mindful athetes through the building of character, self-discipline, and leadership.

General Information

The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athetes residing in Chicago's northside neighborhoods an environment in which to lear and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4 -12 and reside in a Chicago northside neightborhood.NYSL in ran by a small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
